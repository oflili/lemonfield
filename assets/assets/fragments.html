1. Interface Fragments

Function: Short in-game prompts that shape atmosphere through environmental hints rather than explicit instructions. These create curiosity-driven engagement that increases session length and encourages players to explore rather than rush.

System Logic: Fragments dynamically trigger based on proximity, player actions, and fluctuating psychological states (sanity).

● “Whisper to those flowers, they will give you an answer”

Implementation: Proximity trigger. Text fades in UI corner for 3 seconds.

System Response: Mic input → flowers bloom → hidden path revealed.

Impact: Voice experimentation. World feels responsive and alive.

● “A reflection with different eyes, don't stare too long”

Implementation: Mirror approach trigger. Whispered audio with reverb, text appears in reflection.

System Response: Stare 5+ seconds → reflection moves independently. Continue → sanity drop or threat spawns.

Impact: Tension through curiosity. Risk/reward observation gameplay.
