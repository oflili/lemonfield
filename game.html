Title: The Last Chapter

Status: Narrative prototype / Interactive fiction

Technical Contribution

  • ● Systems Design: Built the branching structure and choice logic within Twine (Harlowe).
  • ● Full Narrative Suite: Wrote and edited all passages, focusing on atmospheric pressure and psychological states.
  • ● Asset Creation:Designed custom visual integrations (handwritten letters) and composed original ambient music (BandLab).
  • ● UX/Presentation: Developed basic styling to ensure the interface reflects the narrative's tone.

Design Intent

The experience is designed to be intentionally disorienting. Choices do not always yield clarity, mirroring the core theme of unreliable perception. The looping structure reinforces a meta-narrative: the story begins before the player realizes they are already trapped within it.
